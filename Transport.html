SUSTAINABLE TRANSPORT

Transport is now a fundamental part of life as it is associated with almost everything we do. However, with transportation comes a major problem: the over consumption of resources such as fossil fuels, coals and petrol, leading to adverse effects on our planet.

It is estimated that every year various forms of transport consume between 20% and 25% of the world's energy and this significantly contributes to the increasingly high levels of greenhouse gases that continue to be released into the atmosphere.
Transport is the biggest source of air and noise pollution in the world with air pollution causing 1 in 8 deaths worldwide
In 2014, road transport was by far the biggest emitter of greenhouse gasses, accounting for more than 70% of all GHG emissions from transport.
